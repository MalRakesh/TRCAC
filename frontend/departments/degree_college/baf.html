B.Com (Accounting & Finance)

A 3-year undergraduate program affiliated to the University of Mumbai, designed to meet the growing demand for professionally certified accountants and finance executives in the global market.

About the Course

The Department of Accounting & Finance (B.A.F.) program at TRCAC is a 3-year undergraduate degree designed to meet the increasing demand for professionally certified accountants and finance executives in the global market.

The course curriculum has been practically designed to ensure students are well-prepared for:

  • Chartered Accountancy (CA)
  • Cost and Works Accountancy (CMA)
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Finance and Taxation
  • Banking and Corporate Finance

This course equips students with strong foundation in accounting principles, financial management, and taxation laws, preparing them for both further studies and direct entry into the corporate world.
