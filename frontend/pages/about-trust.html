About Thakur Educational Trust

A young sapling planted initially has blossomed into a tree spreading its branches and stems of education — an example is Thakur Educational Trust.

Thakur Educational Trust was formed by the Thakur family as a sense of responsibility of repaying the social debt. The trust was registered as a Public Trust in the year 1990 with an objective to lend a helping hand towards the welfare of the society and reform the educational system.

Today, it includes top institutions like Thakur Bhagwanidevi Musafir Public School, Thakur Global Business School, Thakur College of Science & Commerce, and more. All institutions under this trust are ISO-certified.
