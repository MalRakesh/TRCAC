Technical education introduced at undergraduate level.

Thakur Institute of Aviation Technology

Pioneering steps in aviation technology education.

Global standards in education set through this institute.

2020

Thakur Global Business School

World-class business management education introduced.

Thakur Bhagwanidevi Musafir Public School

Largest public school project of the trust so far.
