History of TRCAC

Thakur Ramnarayan College of Arts & Commerce was established in 1990 with a vision to provide quality education in the field of Arts and Commerce. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most respected institutions in Mumbai.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, creativity, and ethics. We aim to produce global-ready graduates who can contribute meaningfully to society and industry.

Our Faculty

The college boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members dedicated to nurturing student potential and providing a holistic learning experience.
