Thakur Ramnarayan College of Arts & Commerce

The college is a comprehensive system where individuals are able to interact with their peers in a deep, meaningful way both inside and outside of the classrooms.

History of TRCAC

Thakur Ramnarayan College of Arts & Commerce was established in 1990 with a vision to provide quality education in the field of Arts and Commerce. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most respected institutions in Mumbai.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, creativity, and ethics. We aim to produce global-ready graduates who can contribute meaningfully to society and industry.

Our Faculty

The college boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members dedicated to nurturing student potential and providing a holistic learning experience.

Learning Communities

Students shall engage with many learning communities while at TRCAC: when they join with their entering class in courses that build their skills and sense of opportunity; they develop close ties through their major; and connect to local and national communities through internships, service learning, and community service.

Broad curricular choices encourage students to explore personal interests, meet diverse groups across campus, or prepare for specific graduate and professional programs.
