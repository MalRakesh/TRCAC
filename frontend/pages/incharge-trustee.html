Incharge Trustee's Message

“Don't limit your challenges, challenge your limits.”

We at Thakur Ramnarayan College of Arts and Commerce aim to discover, enroot and delve into the hidden talents and magic lying dormant inside our students. This will be achieved only when we move forward hand-in-hand with sincerity, purpose and dedication to achieve heights of glory.

From academics to co-curricular activities, perseverance and mindfulness are entrenched in the hearts of each student, making them academically distinct and brilliant human beings.

— Shri Jitendra R. Singh Incharge Trustee
