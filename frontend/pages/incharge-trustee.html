Incharge Trustee's Message

“Don't limit your challenges, challenge your limits.”

We at Thakur Ramnarayan College of Arts and Commerce aim to discover, enroot and delve into the hidden talents and magic lying dormant inside all of our students. This will be achieved only when we move forward hand-in-hand with sincerity, purpose and dedication to achieve heights of glory.

From academics to co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, perseverance and mindfulness are entrenched in the hearts of each student, not only making them academically distinct but brilliant human beings.

— Shri Jitendra R. Singh
Incharge Trustee
