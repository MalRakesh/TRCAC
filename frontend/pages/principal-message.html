Principal's Message

“Education is not just about learning facts, but about developing the wisdom to apply them for a better world.”

For the past decade, Thakur Ramnarayan College of Arts and Commerce has scaled several heights in delivering peerless education and creating a humane, inclusive society.

Our Vision is to reach excellence through academic and research capabilities. Our team of highly inspired and motivated faculty members will fuel the institution’s ascent on the trajectory to success.

As educators, it is our moral duty to guide and support students to achieve their academic and personal goals. We believe in equipping our students with modern, relevant skill sets so they attain professional success and become responsible global citizens.

— Dr. Sumathi Rajkumar
Principal
