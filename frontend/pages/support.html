Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?
Visit the Admission page and fill the online form with required documents like 10th/12th marksheet, ID proof, and passport-size photo.
What are the office hours?
Our office is open from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Closed on Sundays and public holidays.
Do you offer online classes?
Yes, we offer hybrid learning with recorded lectures and live sessions for enrolled students.
How can I get placement support?
Our Placement Cell provides resume building, mock interviews, and direct access to company drives.
